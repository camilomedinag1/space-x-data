üéØ Introduction

Problem Statement

SpaceX's competitive advantage lies in its ability to reuse the first stage of Falcon 9 rockets, reducing launch costs from $165M+ to $62M. Predicting first stage landing success is crucial for:

  • Cost estimation and competitive bidding
  • Mission planning and risk assessment
  • Operational optimization
  • Strategic decision making

Project Objectives

  • Analyze historical SpaceX launch data
  • Identify key factors affecting landing success
  • Develop predictive models for landing outcomes
  • Create interactive visualizations and dashboards
  • Provide actionable business insights
